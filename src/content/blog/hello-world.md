This is where your first blog post would go. Write it in Markdown, add frontmatter with title, date, tags, and description, and it will automatically appear on the blog.

## What to include

- **Frontmatter** at the top with metadata
- **Markdown content** for the article body
- **Images** in `public/images/` and reference as `/images/photo.png`

## Example structure

```markdown
---
title: "My Post Title"
description: "A short description for SEO"
date: "2025-07-01"
tags: ["Tag1", "Tag2"]
featured: true
readingTime: "5"
---

Your article content here...
```
